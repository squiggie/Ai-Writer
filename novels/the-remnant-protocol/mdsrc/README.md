# The Remnant Protocol

**Genre:** Literary Dystopia  
**In the tradition of:** Kazuo Ishiguro, Cormac McCarthy, Walter M. Miller Jr., George Orwell

## Premise
In the near-future city of Vael, Caleb — a meticulous AI systems architect — discovers a null reference buried in the city's compliance infrastructure that should not exist. Following it means risking everything: his clearance, his career, his daughter's safety. Not following it means being complicit in something he cannot yet name. The novel tracks his methodical unraveling of a system designed to be unquestionable, and the quiet grace that waits on the other side of obedience to conscience over institution.

## About this novel
Faith operates quietly — through preserved objects, through choices made without witness, through the question Caleb cannot stop asking: whether what he is doing matters if no one ever knows. The novel's answer is provisional and earned, not preached.
