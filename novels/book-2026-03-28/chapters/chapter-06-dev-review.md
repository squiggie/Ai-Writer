## Developmental Review: Chapter 6

### Structural Assessment
This chapter lands cleanly in the novel's "Go" phase. Mara stops merely withholding institutional permission and actively pursues new evidence outside the approved lane, which is the correct escalation after Chapter 5's private refusal. The chapter architecture is strong: risky skiff descent, near-failure that sharpens both danger and data value, then lab interpretation that converts collection into story consequence. The single scene break is justified because it marks both a location shift and a change in pressure from physical peril to analytical/moral recognition. Pacing is mostly effective: the skiff sequence moves briskly and feels earned, while the lab half slows enough to parse the evidence. The main structural weakness is that the chapter's final realization arrives slightly more as conceptual conclusion than as fully dramatized emotional turn. Because this is a key threshold chapter, the last movement could afford another small degree of slowing in Mara's body and immediate moral apprehension before the closing formulation.

### Character Arc Status
Mara is progressing convincingly from procedural restraint toward costly stewardship. Her choice to exploit the maintenance envelope is an externalized version of the inner shift established in Chapter 5, so the arc is tracking logically. Her wound remains active in useful ways: she is still careful with nouns, still resisting overclaiming, still trying to keep evidence ahead of meaning. That restraint is character-true and gives the chapter credibility. The strongest arc beat is not the skiff maneuver itself but her refusal to let Serran's categories define what the evidence is allowed to become. What is still somewhat underplayed is the embodied emotional cost of crossing this line. We see fatigue, pain, grip, breathing, and rigid shoulders, but in the lab revelation sequence Mara sometimes returns to analytic language so quickly that the personal transformation can feel half a beat ahead of the body's registration of it.

### World-Building Notes
The hard-science frame is one of the chapter's major strengths. The maintenance skiff, sample logic, particulate collection design, plume behavior, and command workaround all feel grounded in the established station ecology. The chapter usefully expands the anomaly from signal behavior into matter transport, which is a meaningful escalation rather than repetitive confirmation. It also preserves internal consistency: Enceladus's intelligence is still inferential, mediated through pressure, fracture, mineral state, and transport constraints rather than suddenly becoming legible in a soft or mystical way. The command logic also remains believable: "remote only" as a means of preserving ambiguity fits Serran's institutional posture. No world-building contradictions stood out.

### Thematic Resonance
The chapter advances the novel's central stewardship theme effectively by making the question less "is there a signal?" and more "what does it mean if matter itself is being patterned?" That deepens the moral stakes without forcing a speech. Thematically, the strongest move is the insistence that encounter remains physical and evidentiary. The chapter also preserves the novel's Christian-adjacent moral grammar of responsibility without becoming explicit or didactic; it stays in witness, naming, and obligation rather than abstract sermonizing. The ending line of thought is strong because it reframes the plume as medium rather than resource. Still, this thematic landing would hit harder if Mara's bodily response and immediate dread/reverence were allowed a little more space before the final declarative paragraph.

### Bestseller Compliance
The chapter opens with immediate motion and a clear mission, sustains tension through operational risk, and ends with a genuine escalation in the anomaly's significance. The hook-to-payoff structure is commercially effective: dangerous sortie, plausible procedural deception, narrowly managed failure, then a discovery that materially changes the case against extraction. The chapter ending does produce forward momentum into Chapter 7. Dialogue also generally avoids static exposition because Linh's interruptions keep the scene alive and adversarial. The chapter is less strong in one expected reader-payoff area: this is a major revelation chapter, and readers will likely want the emotional beat to dilate slightly more at the point where Mara understands the plume is carrying arrangement, not only chemistry. Right now the chapter is compelling, but it could become more unforgettable by dwelling a fraction longer in the bodily shock of that recognition.

### Strengths
- The chapter clearly advances both plot and proof; it does not feel like redundant validation.
- The skiff sequence has strong procedural texture and believable operational stakes.
- Mara's voice matches the style guide well: exact, disciplined, technical first, emotion implied through observation.
- Linh is used effectively as pressure, contrast, and human friction rather than exposition delivery alone.
- Physical beats are present in dialogue scenes: service cap, breathing control, hands on the bench, rigid shoulders, tool sounds, vibration, and harness pressure all keep bodies on the page.
- The scene break is warranted and functional rather than evasive.
- The final conceptual escalation from signal to transport to arrangement is strong and novel-specific.

### Concerns
- The lab-half pacing is solid procedurally, but the chapter's most important emotional turn is still somewhat compressed; Mara arrives at the implication quickly once the data aligns.
- Mara's body is present throughout the sortie, but during the final recognition sequence the prose sometimes shifts back into abstraction before fully letting the body register encounter.
- A few lines lean slightly more literary-polished than Mara's controlled scientific voice, especially when summarizing realization in elevated terms; the risk is subtle drift from disciplined first-person analysis into authorial flourish.
- Linh's dialogue is lively and useful, but a few exchanges verge on reiterating the same caution-that-isn't-caution dynamic rather than escalating it.

### Suggestions
- Slow the final lab realization by one additional beat in Mara's body before the closing paragraph: breath, pulse, nausea, hand position, or a small involuntary action would deepen the emotional weight without violating her restraint.
- Trim or consolidate one or two dialogue turns in the back half where Linh pushes Mara to name the implication; keep the sharpest versions and remove repetition so each exchange escalates rather than circles.
- Review the last two pages for phrases that sound more like retrospective thematic narration than Mara's immediate processing, and recast them into her preferred mode of observation -> qualification -> implication.
- Consider giving the overdue command prompt a slightly stronger dramatic function in the closing movement so the institutional threat presses more directly against the moment of recognition.
